Mid Semester Project description
The goal of this mini project was to analyse the relationship between educational attainment and income in the macro counties in Iowa. Macro counties are counties that have a population of above 50,000. The macro counties were identifed by mapping out counties in Iowa with population of over 50,000. 11 Counties were identified. The data on Wages and Salaries and Per capita income were obtained from te regional ecoonomic accounts website and data on the educational attainment was obtained from census website. The analysis incicated that, counties with high educational attainments had a high wages and salaries.
